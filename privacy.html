Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 16, 2007

Track Attack Running, LLC ("Track Attack Running," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: name, email address, and message content submitted via our contact form.
  • Usage data: basic analytics such as page views, referrers, and approximate location derived from IP (aggregated).
  • Cookies: small files used to remember preferences and understand site usage.

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide coaching-related communication.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website, including troubleshooting and analytics.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, or security risks.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK visitors)

  • Consent (e.g., when you submit the contact form).
  • Legitimate interests (website analytics, security, service improvement).
  • Legal obligation (where required by law).

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate the site (e.g., hosting, analytics), bound by confidentiality and data protection terms, or as required by law.

Cookies & Analytics

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not function properly without cookies. We may use privacy‑friendly analytics that aggregate data and avoid tracking you across sites.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Messages sent via the contact form may be retained for recordkeeping.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please use the Contact Us form.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

Your information may be processed in the United States. By using the site, you consent to the transfer of information to the U.S. and the application of U.S. laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to submit a privacy request, please use the Contact Us form.
